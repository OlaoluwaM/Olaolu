The sport enforcers quest

Poverty and Gender inequality in sport

Our group, the sports enforcers set out to find out how gender and poverty inequality affect sports participation and what we can do to help.

We aim to educate people in knowing the benefits of participating in sports and recreation while encouraging female participation in sports. We would also like make people make people aware of the limitations (like poverty) in sports participation and better enable them to make a difference.
